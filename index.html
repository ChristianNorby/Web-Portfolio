US Electric Utility Rates 2021

POWER BI
In this project, I transformed a public raw dataset of U.S. utility energy rates into an interactive Power BI dashboard. I connected and cleaned the data, built calculated measures, and designed visualizations to highlight key insights. The dashboard includes: Average residential, commercial, and industrial rates by state displayed on a map. KPI cards summarizing national averages and trends. Top 5 states table using DAX measures for comparison. Clustered bar charts showing ownership vs. average rate differences. Interactive slicers and drill-downs to filter by sector or region. This project demonstrates my ability to combine data modeling, DAX calculations, and visualization design to deliver insights that connect the energy industry with financial and operational perspectives..
